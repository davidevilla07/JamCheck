Our History


The buying and selling of used car in Jamaica have been considered a US multi-million-dollar industry. This has caused the spawn of numerous used car dealer businesses. These dealers are benefiting greatly from the industry. But the question at hand is, do buyers of these used cars benefit as well? Buying a used car in Jamaica, especially if it has been previously owned by someone in Jamaica is a great risk. Imagine buying a car that was repaired after it met in a serious accident in Jamaica. How would you the new owner know that the used car you just purchased met in serious accident? Used car purchaser in Jamaica, especially those young and naïve see a car that they like and without making an informed decision, purchase a car that would drain their pockets with repairs soon after owning it. These are some of the factors new used car owners fail to consider before purchasing:

● Did the car meet in accident before?

● Was the being serviced properly by the previous owner?

● Were there any major repairs done to the car?

● Was the car reported stolen?

● How many owners did the car have?

We now welcome JamCheck.com. The objective of this website would operate just like the notable CARFAX in the United States. Each car that enters the island would be logged using the Chassis/Vin Number, Engine Number, Colour Code and Transmission Number through Jamaica Customs. This website would be linked with every Car Insurance Company in Jamaica, the Tax Administration of Jamaica, the Transport Authority and the Police Report System. Some of the functions of JamCheck.com include but limited

● Searching for a vehicle


● Check vehicle details (Colour, Chassis/vin number, transmission number)


● Check for number of previous owners


● Check for importation date


● Check for previous and current Insurers


● Store pictures of the car each time it gets insured and before being sold


● Check for police records if ever stolen


● Check for any major repairs (colour change, body repairs, engine/transmission repairs)


● Check servicing dates
